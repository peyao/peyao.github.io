Verdict

  • Pros
  • Bigger, beautiful screen.
  • Still shots are improved from last generation.
  • Video is immensly improved over last generation. Slow-mo for days.
  • New body, though a throwback to the first gen, is elegant, thin, and clean.
  • Beatiful revamp to the OS, and made customization much more flexible.
  • Cons
  • Loyalist users may find the size a large relative to older models.
  • Battery life is about the same or worse than last gen- would have rather had that over a thinner phone.
  • Feels like a bar of soap, durability may become an issue.
